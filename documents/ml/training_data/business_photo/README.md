# Business Photo - Training Data

## What Goes Here
Photos of actual business premises, stores, or workspaces.

## Philippine MSME Business Examples
- Sari-sari store (neighborhood convenience store)
- Market stall/tiangge
- Carinderia/eatery
- Home-based business setup
- Tricycle/jeepney (for transport operators)
- Small manufacturing workshop
- Street food cart
- Online seller inventory/workspace
- Barbershop/salon
- Repair shop

## Image Requirements
- **Format:** JPG, JPEG, or PNG
- **Minimum Size:** 224 x 224 pixels
- **Quality:** Business clearly visible
- **Content:** Storefront, stall, or workspace showing business activity

## Target Count
**30-50 images minimum**

## Where to Find
1. **Google Images:** Search "sari-sari store Philippines"
2. **Google Images:** Search "tiangge stall Philippines"
3. **Google Images:** Search "carinderia Philippines"
4. **Google Maps:** Street view of local stores
5. **Take Photos:** Visit local markets, neighborhoods (with permission)
6. **Stock Photos:** Filipino small business images

## Variations to Include
- Different business types (retail, food, services)
- Indoor and outdoor businesses
- Day and night shots
- Various signage styles
- Busy and quiet times
- Clean and cluttered setups

## Sample Search Terms
- "sari-sari store Philippines"
- "Filipino market vendor stall"
- "carinderia eatery Philippines"
- "home based business Philippines"
- "small business Philippines MSME"
- "tiangge market stall"
- "tindahan Philippines"

## Easy to Collect
This category is easiest to collect locally:
1. Walk around your neighborhood
2. Take photos of sari-sari stores, market stalls
3. Ask permission from store owners
4. Photos should show the business setup clearly

## ⚠️ Privacy Note
- Ask permission before photographing businesses
- Avoid capturing faces of customers
- Blur any visible personal information
