# Income Proof - Training Data

## What Goes Here
Documents that show income or financial transactions.

## Philippine Income Proof Examples
- Bank statements (BDO, BPI, Metrobank, Landbank, etc.)
- GCash transaction history/screenshot
- Maya (PayMaya) transaction history
- Official receipts from business
- Sales logbook/record book
- ITR (Income Tax Return) - Form 1701
- Certificate of Employment with salary
- Remittance receipts (Western Union, Palawan Express)
- Payment vouchers
- Payslip/pay stub

## Image Requirements
- **Format:** JPG, JPEG, or PNG
- **Minimum Size:** 224 x 224 pixels
- **Quality:** Text/numbers legible
- **Content:** Financial document showing transactions or income

## Target Count
**30-50 images minimum**

## Where to Find
1. **Google Images:** Search "bank statement sample Philippines"
2. **Google Images:** Search "GCash transaction history screenshot"
3. **Google Images:** Search "BDO bank statement sample"
4. **Google Images:** Search "official receipt Philippines sample"
5. **Stock Sites:** Financial document templates
6. **Screenshots:** Create fake GCash/Maya screens (for demo only)

## Variations to Include
- Different banks (BDO, BPI, Metrobank, Landbank)
- E-wallet screenshots (GCash, Maya)
- Handwritten sales logbooks
- Official receipts
- Different date formats
- Mobile app screenshots vs printed statements

## Sample Search Terms
- "BDO bank statement sample"
- "GCash transaction history"
- "Maya PayMaya statement"
- "Philippine bank statement template"
- "Official receipt sample Philippines"
- "ITR form 1701 sample"

## For MSME Context
Many microentrepreneurs don't have formal bank statements.
Include informal income proof like:
- Handwritten sales records
- Simple receipt books
- GCash/Maya as primary "bank"

## ⚠️ Privacy Note
- Use sample/template documents
- Blur account numbers and personal details
- Never use real financial data
- Create fake screenshots for training purposes only
