# Business Permit - Training Data

## What Goes Here
Business registration and permit documents.

## Philippine Business Document Examples
- DTI Certificate of Business Name Registration
- Mayor's Permit / Business Permit
- Barangay Business Clearance
- SEC Registration Certificate
- BIR Certificate of Registration (Form 2303)
- Cooperative Development Authority (CDA) Registration

## Image Requirements
- **Format:** JPG, JPEG, or PNG
- **Minimum Size:** 224 x 224 pixels
- **Quality:** Document text should be legible
- **Content:** Full document visible

## Target Count
**30-50 images minimum**

## Where to Find
1. **Google Images:** Search "DTI certificate sample Philippines"
2. **Google Images:** Search "Mayor's permit sample Philippines"
3. **Google Images:** Search "Barangay business clearance sample"
4. **Government Websites:** Sample templates from DTI, LGU sites
5. **Stock Document Sites:** Template examples

## Variations to Include
- DTI certificates (most common for MSMEs)
- Mayor's permits from different municipalities
- Barangay clearances (different barangay formats)
- Old and new format permits
- Different paper colors (white, yellow, official paper)
- Framed vs unframed documents

## Sample Search Terms
- "DTI certificate of registration sample"
- "Mayor's permit template Philippines"
- "Barangay business permit sample"
- "SEC certificate sample Philippines"
- "Business permit Philippines example"

## ⚠️ Privacy Note
- Use sample/template documents
- Blur business names and addresses if using real permits
- Avoid documents with TIN or other sensitive numbers
