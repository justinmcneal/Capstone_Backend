# Proof of Address - Training Data

## What Goes Here
Documents that verify a person's residential address.

## Philippine Proof of Address Examples
- Electric Bill (Meralco, Visayan Electric, etc.)
- Water Bill (Manila Water, Maynilad, local water district)
- Barangay Certificate of Residency
- Phone/Internet Bill (PLDT, Globe, Converge)
- Bank Statement (with address)
- Lease Contract / Rental Agreement
- Property Tax Receipt
- Voter's Registration Confirmation

## Image Requirements
- **Format:** JPG, JPEG, or PNG
- **Minimum Size:** 224 x 224 pixels
- **Quality:** Address section visible
- **Content:** Utility bill or certificate showing name and address

## Target Count
**30-50 images minimum**

## Where to Find
1. **Google Images:** Search "Meralco bill sample"
2. **Google Images:** Search "Manila Water bill sample"
3. **Google Images:** Search "Barangay certificate of residency sample"
4. **Google Images:** Search "utility bill Philippines sample"
5. **Stock Photo Sites:** Utility bill templates

## Variations to Include
- Different utility providers (Meralco, Manila Water, PLDT)
- Barangay certificates (different formats per LGU)
- Old and new bill formats
- Paper bills and printed e-bills
- Different paper sizes
- Partial and full bill images

## Sample Search Terms
- "Meralco electric bill sample Philippines"
- "Manila Water bill sample"
- "Barangay certificate of residency template"
- "PLDT bill sample"
- "Philippine utility bill example"

## ⚠️ Privacy Note
- Use sample/template bills
- Blur account numbers and exact addresses
- Avoid real customer information
