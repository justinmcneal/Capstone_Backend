PROJECT INFORMATION
RESEARCH TITLE
MSME Pathways: Smart Loan Support for the Informal Sector
TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION
RATIONALE
Millions of Filipino microentrepreneurs—such as sari-sari store owners, market vendors, home-based sellers, and informal workers—remain financially excluded due to a lack of financial literacy, distrust of formal institutions, and minimal access to bank services. Traditional microfinance outreach is labor-intensive and difficult to scale, leaving many potential borrowers unaware of safer, responsible alternatives to informal lending.
This project addresses the gap by developing an AI-enabled system that guides microentrepreneurs through loan education, pre-screening, and alternative data profiling to help them understand and access formal financial support. The problem persists due to digital barriers, low literacy levels, and outdated outreach models, making an intelligent and scalable solution necessary today.
This project aligns with:
SDG 1: No Poverty (promoting financial resilience)


SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth (supporting MSME growth and access to capital)


SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure (building inclusive financial technology solutions)
OBJECTIVES
To develop an AI-driven financial guidance system that explains loan concepts in simple, culturally relevant language for microentrepreneurs.


To design a smart pre-qualification and alternative data profiling engine that supports ethical lending decisions for users with little to no credit history.


To evaluate the system’s effectiveness in improving user financial understanding, loan readiness, and confidence in formal lending processes.
METHODOLOGY
Data Gathering
 Conduct interviews, surveys, and small-group discussions with microentrepreneurs to understand financial behavior, pain points, and user needs.


System Design
 Create UI/UX wireframes, flow diagrams, and system architecture covering mobile interfaces and web interfaces.


Development
Implement AI chatbot features, document recognition models, and profiling modules using related technologies.


Testing & Evaluation
 Conduct usability testing, debugging, and refinement based on user feedback and system performance metrics.

A functional prototype of the Smart Loan Support System

POTENTIAL IMPACT
Expands access to responsible loan information and safe financial pathways for underserved microentrepreneurs.


Strengthens financial literacy and confidence among users, reducing dependence on informal lenders.
